12.24 Yield Management.
An airline is selling tickets for flights to a particular destination. The flight will depart in three weeks' time. It can use up to six planes each costing £50 000 to hire. Each plane has:
37
First Class seats
38
Business Class seats
47
Economy Class seats

Up to 10% of seats in any one categoty can be transferred to an adjacent category.
It wishes to decide a price for each of these seats. There will be further opportunities to update these prices in after one week and two weeks. Once a customer has purchased a ticket there is no cancellation option.
