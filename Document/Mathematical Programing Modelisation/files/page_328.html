14.21 Agricultural Pricing.
The optimal prices are
Milk
£303 per ton
Butter
£667 per ton
Cheese 1
£900 per ton
Cheese 2
£1085 per ton

The resultant yearly revenue will be £1992m. It is straightforward to calculate the yearly demands which will result from these prices. They are
Milk
4781 000 tons
Butter
384 000 tons
Cheese 1
250 000 tons
Cheese 2
57 000 tons

The economic cost of imposing a constraint on the price index can be obtained from the shadow price on the constraint. For this example this shadow price in the optimal solution indicates that each £1 by which the new prices are allowed to increase the cost of last year's consumption would result in an increased revenue of £0.61.
